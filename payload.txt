REM AutoCorrect changer prnk
REM inspired by someone else that made something similar to this but it never worked for me for some reason, so i decided to make my own #
REM does not need any administrator permissions as far as im aware as this does not use things like cmd or powershell.
REM it is reccomended to change the word/letter and the phrase.


REM this part just opens up word with the windows search bar
DELAY 1000
GUI
DELAY 500
STRING word
DELAY 1000
ENTER

REM we are now going to the area that we need to change the autocorrect
DELAY 5000
ALT Q
DELAY 500
T
DELAY 500
DOWN
DELAY 100
DOWN
DELAY 100
TAB
DELAY 100
ENTER

REM this is the main part here, id reccomend changing any letter/word to anything in this area
REM to change things, replace the 'i' to a word or letter and then change the other string to whatever you want it to replace to.
DELAY 500
STRING i
DELAY 100
TAB
DELAY 100
STRING i really do not want to go back to school
DELAY 1000

ENTER
DELAY 1000
ENTER
DELAY 1000
ENTER
DELAY 1000
ALT F4
DELAY 500
ALT F4
